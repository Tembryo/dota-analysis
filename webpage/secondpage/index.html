About Wisdota

Wisdota is an automated coaching platform for Dota players. Our machine learning algorithms can analyse your replay files and pinpoint ways in which you can improve.

Personalised Analysis

Analyse your Dota games and compare to top players. See the patterns in your play from last hitting performance to fights.

Replay Storage

All of your replays automatically downloaded and stored for life. Wisdota makes filtering and sorting through your replays quick and easy.

Team Strategy

Annotate and share summaries of matches with teammates to discuss tactics. Strategise like the top pros and win more matches.
